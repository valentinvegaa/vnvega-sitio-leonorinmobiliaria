Quitaqui

One hour from the city of Valdivia, in the town of Quitaqui, we offer plots starting at 5000 m2, which are the perfect escape from the city towards a clean and natural environment.

By terrestrial or fluvial access, we can access the Quitaqui valley, which perfectly blends the plains of its geography with wooded lands and wetlands. Quitaqui gathers everything you can expect from nature in one place, a great variety of typical species of local fauna, shrubs and trees both native and fruit trees.

The plots that we offer are surrounded by natural reserves such as the Oncol Park, the Rio Cruces, Llenehue and Pilunkura wetlands. In addition, being a town famous for the exploitation of sports tourism such as Mountain Bike and Trekking.

Tres Chiflones

It is a town that belongs to the commune of Corral. This location belongs to the farthest from the center of the commune of Corral, but the distance is part of the natural landscape that amaze visitors, rest and observation of flora and fauna, as well as the possibility of having a picnic on the banks of the river or In the middle of the forest.

Within the 110 hectares that we offer our clients, 20 are native forest. We have around 6 pure water springs, explorable trails between the native vegetation of the area, a wide variety of birds and native animals, various species of fruit trees such as Calafate, Murta, wild Strawberry, among others. Our group of plots adjoins the local road T-640, approximately 8 kilometers of gravel, where it crosses the T-60 road, 2 kilometers from the Futa River (Tornagaleones).
